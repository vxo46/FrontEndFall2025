Florida Bigfoot

  • Also known as: Skunk Ape
  • Habitat: Everglades
  • Fun fact: Loves Publix chicken tender subs

Legend has it, Florida Bigfoot roams the Everglades at dusk. Locals say you can hear him rustling palmetto bushes, always vanishing before anyone gets a clear picture. The Skunk Ape’s favorite trick? Stealing sandwiches from unsuspecting campers.

Florida Man

  • Rides inflatable flamingos into hurricanes
  • Uses fireworks as bug spray
  • Chaos magnet: Always in the news

Florida Man once made headlines for attempting to cook a frozen pizza on the hood of his car in July. When asked why, he simply replied, “The sun was free.” His adventures rarely end well, but always make the news.

Florida Woman

  • Argues with raccoons for Doritos
  • Karaoke airboat parties
  • Bedazzled emergency gear

Florida Woman once stopped traffic on Alligator Alley to rescue a baby gator she swore winked at her. Witnesses recall her singing karaoke to calm it down—naturally, the song was “Eye of the Tiger.”
