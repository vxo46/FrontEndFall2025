Florida Bigfoot

  • Also known as: Skunk Ape
  • Habitat: Everglades
  • Fun fact: Loves Publix chicken tender subs

Locals say Florida Bigfoot appears at dusk, lumbering through the sawgrass with a sandwich in hand. Some claim he leaves behind a trail of swamp water and laughter, but no one’s ever caught him on camera long enough to prove it.

Florida Man

  • Rides inflatable flamingos into hurricanes
  • Uses fireworks as bug spray
  • Chaos magnet: Always in the news

Florida Man’s misadventures are legendary: one week he’s arrested for wrestling an alligator in a convenience store parking lot, the next he’s spotted parasailing with a lawn chair. His motto? “If it makes the news, it was worth it.”

Florida Woman

  • Argues with raccoons for Doritos
  • Karaoke airboat parties
  • Bedazzled emergency gear

Florida Woman once held up traffic to save a baby alligator, all while belting out karaoke classics. She’s equally famous for her sequined survival kits and spontaneous dance-offs at roadside fruit stands.

FAQ

Are they real? The legends are real. The evidence is flexible.
